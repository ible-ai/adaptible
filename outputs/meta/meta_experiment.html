Interpreting the Meta-Learning Score

The meta-learning score measures how learning efficiency changes over time:

  • Score > 0: Model is "learning to learn" - improvements accelerate and forgetting decelerates
  • Score < 0: Model is degrading - improvements slow down, forgetting increases
  • High variance: Meta-learning ability is sensitive to initialization

Formula: (late_improvement_rate - early_improvement_rate) + (early_forgetting_rate - late_forgetting_rate)
